THE TRADITIONAL DEALERSHIP
MARKETING MODEL IS BROKEN.

Listing sites are a tax, not a strategy.

Paying thousands monthly to AutoTrader and Cars.com puts you in a race against every dealer in your market. You're paying to compete, not to win.

Generic agencies don't understand inventory.

Most agencies treat your dealership like any other business. They don't know how fast inventory moves or how quickly an ad becomes irrelevant when a car sells.

Campaigns set up once and reviewed monthly waste 30-40% of spend on vehicles that already sold, audiences that already saw the ad, or creative that stopped working weeks ago.

Reporting tells you what happened, not what to do.

Most dealers get monthly PDFs full of impressions and clicks. We give you real-time leads, attributed to real vehicles, with actions you can take today.

YOUR ADS RUN
THEMSELVES.

We deploy a performance-driven system into your ad account. It works autonomously around the clock — no account managers, no manual updates, no delays.

01

Inventory Feed Connected

We connect to your live inventory feed. Every vehicle in stock becomes an active ad automatically — new arrivals added, sold cars removed, prices updated in real time.

02

Campaigns Built Automatically

Ad copy is written, audiences are selected, budgets are set, and campaigns launch — all without human input. Every ad is precision-targeted to in-market buyers in your area.
