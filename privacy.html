PRIVACY
POLICY.

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Calibr Media LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

When you use our services or work with us as a client, we may collect:

  • Business contact information (name, email, phone, dealership name)
  • Advertising account access (Facebook, Instagram, Google) when granted by you
  • Vehicle inventory data from your dealership website or feed
  • Campaign performance data and analytics
  • Communications between you and Calibr Media

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide advertising management services for your dealership
  • Create, manage, and optimize Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns
  • Sync your live inventory with advertising platforms
  • Generate performance reports and analytics
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Process payments and manage billing

Information Sharing

We do not sell your information. We may share information with:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook/Instagram) for advertising delivery
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • SendGrid for email communications
  • Service providers who help us operate our business

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure data storage, and access controls.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your advertising accounts at any time
